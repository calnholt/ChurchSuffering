Waystation — Collection Overlay (3 Variants)

Replaces the Saints Medal Chronicle modal with a tabbed player collection: SAINTS keeps every feature of the old modal (medal grid, locked slots, saint detail with keyword blocks, progress meter), plus new CARDS and EQUIPMENT tabs. Cards use the in-game frame, fan like the booster showcase, and clicking a card cycles the viewed color (White → Red → Black; black shows +1 BLK). A dashed "+" tab reserves room for future tabs.

Variant 1 — Reliquary Wall

Candlelit gold chapel chrome. Arched tabs hang under the title like reliquary alcoves. Saints keep the split wall/altar layout; cards sit in fanned pew rows grouped by kind; equipment fills four slot columns with the in-game slot icons. Hover anything: it lifts toward the candlelight.
