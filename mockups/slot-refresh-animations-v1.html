Slot Refresh Animations

Conveyor belt enter/exit for timed shop refreshes and encounter rotation on the meta layer. Slots exit left and replacements enter from the right — same motion for shop and encounters.

Conveyor Belt

Horizontal Slot Swap

Expired slots slide out to the left while replacements march in from the right. Shop and encounter columns share the same axis and stagger timing.

Exit: -X left
Enter: +X right
Stagger: 70ms

Implementation Notes

  • Shop refresh swaps all shop slots at once when the global refresh timer fires.
  • Enemy expiry rotates one slot independently when its leave timer hits zero.
  • Both use identical conveyor motion: translateX exit left, enter from right.
  • Block pointer input on affected columns for the full animation duration (~700ms).
  • Swap entity data at peak exit (opacity near 0) to avoid visible content pop.
