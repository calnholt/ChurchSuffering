Climb Points Award Overlay

Quick animation overlay shown after a climb ends and the player returns to the Waystation. Points mirror CollectionProgressionRules.CalculateClimbPoints — shop refresh milestones at time 8 / 16 / 24 (+1 / +3 / +5) plus +3 for defeating the final boss. Abandoned climbs award nothing. Banked points appear as pendingClimbPoints until claimed on the Achievements screen.

Implementation notes

Trigger — Publish after ClimbEndedEvent when routing to WayStation (WayStationArrivalKind.ReturnedFromFailedClimb or victory). Overlay blocks POI input until animation completes (~3.6s) or player skips.
Point tiers (cumulative caps from CollectionProgressionRules)
Time ≥ 8 → +1  |  Time ≥ 16 → +3  |  Time ≥ 24 → +5  |  Final boss → +3  |  Abandoned → 0
Animation timeline (scales with earned tiers)
Dim fade  ·  title slam  ·  only earned tiers reveal staggered (~420ms apart)  ·  total slams ~380ms after last tier lands  ·  dismiss after brief hold
System — ClimbPointsAwardOverlayDisplaySystem on WayStation scene; read ClimbEndedEvent payload already persisted to pendingClimbPoints.
