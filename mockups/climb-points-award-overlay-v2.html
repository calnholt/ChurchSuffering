V2 implementation notes

Visual thesis — The reward breakdown now behaves like an ascent. Each earned threshold lights one higher node, making cumulative progress readable before the total resolves.
Motion order — Scene settles, title appears, route grows bottom-to-top, milestone cards land on alternating sides, then the crest slams in with a count-up, ring, and short particle burst.
MonoGame-friendly construction — Background dim/vignette, two frame rules, a center rail, bordered cards, diamond nodes, crest diamonds, expanding ring, and 18 short line particles. Only the existing Waystation image and project fonts are required.
Timing — First tier at 620ms, additional tiers every 410ms, total 520ms after the last tier, 1.35s hold. A player input after the total reveal dismisses immediately; earlier input completes the sequence.
Point rules — Time 8+ = +1 | Time 16+ = +3 | Time 24+ = +5 | Final boss = +3 | Abandoned = 0.
